About Me

Welcome to my Portfolio Page! My name is Malik Henry and I am a mathematician, software engineer, mathematics educator, and musician based out of Atlanta, GA. I graduated in 2018 from the University of Georgia with a Bachelor of Science in Mathematics, Certificate in Actuarial Sciences, and Minor in Music.

As a mathematician by training, my primary interests lie in using software and computational methods as a tool for problem solving and effective interfacing (i.e., making computers do math for me). Recently, this has taken the form of pursuing a Full-stack flex Web Development certification from the Georgia Institute of Technology (expected completion date Auguset 2022). Upon completion of the program, I seek to combine my software skills and my mathematics background to build effective and meaningful technologies.

Outside of work, my interests include philosophy, combat sports, weightlifting, and music.
